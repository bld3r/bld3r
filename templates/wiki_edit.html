Tag Communities
Not just labels

Tags categorize objects with other, similar objects.

Members of tag communities define what a tag means through an open, editable wiki page.

Well-defined tags make it easier for you and others to find relevant objects.

Tag communities decide what objects belong, determine who makes the best objects, and discover tag news.
